Branding

What does the company do/offer?

Among the Clouds (based on Top of Vancouver) is a restaurant looking to offer a unique dining experience. Set over 500 feet above the ground, Among the Clouds features a varied menu of western dishes and beverages to be enjoyed with a panorama view of Vancouver.

Who is the company to potnential customers

Among the Clouds promotes towards tourists and families as a fun and unique dining experience. Ebodied as a person, the brand is a confident friend who expresses themselves through company and laughs. Its clean and relaxing appearance matches the skies the restaurant is located.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

In addition to the attraction itself, the welcoming appearance should not intimidate any and promote inclusivity to any strangers.
